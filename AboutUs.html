About Us

Welcome to MO Mayai! We are dedicated to delivering the freshest, farm-raised eggs right to your doorstep. Our mission is to provide high-quality eggs sourced locally from trusted farms, ensuring you get the best taste and nutrition.

Our Story

MO Mayai was founded in 2024 by Fahima Mohamed, who had a passion for sustainable farming and providing fresh, nutritious eggs to the community. What started as a small family farm has grown into a trusted brand known for its quality and commitment to excellence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver farm-fresh eggs that are not only delicious but also ethically produced. We believe in supporting local farmers and promoting sustainable farming practices to ensure a healthier future for everyone.

Our Farm

Our eggs are sourced from local farms that follow strict hygiene and animal welfare standards. Our hens are raised in a natural and healthy environment, ensuring that every egg is packed with nutrition and flavor.

Community Involvement

We believe in giving back to the community that supports us. MO Mayai is proud to be involved in various local projects, from sponsoring educational programs to participating in environmental conservation efforts.
